Descriptions about Molly


Physical traits




Age: 1 years old
Gender: female

Molly have a blue appearance but there is only a little part that with red colour. It have small dorsal fin, pelvic fin, caudal fin and anal fin.




Characteristics





Molly are generally calm, slow-moving fish who do not require as much space as many other fish. Molly are exceptionally tolerant of low oxygen levels and poor water quality, owing to their special labyrinth organ, a characteristic unique to the suborder Anabantoidei that allows for the intake of surface air.
